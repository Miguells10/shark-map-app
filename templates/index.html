LAUNCH MAP WATCH: WHAT IS SHARK MAP

OUR MISSION:

To protect the delicate balance of the ocean, we must first understand its most vital predators. Our initiative combines NASA's space-based vision with advanced predictive modeling to decode the secrets of shark behavior. Our goal is to answer not only where sharks are, but why they are there and where they are heading next.

THE PROJECT ENABLES:

  • Prediction of critical foraging hotspots.
  • Application of predictive data for real-time conservation.
  • Development of next-generation tracking technology.
